About Us

Welcome to the customers's most beloved cafeteria. We offer a wide range of flavourful drinks and snacks to start or finish your day with. Our speciality coffee has always been the customers's most favourite.


We have been serving our customers for many generations and still going. Your satisfaction is our number one priority. We have are extremely grateful for the support you have been giving us during this pandemic.


We are doing our best to keep everything clean and prepare everything with safety masks on to mitigate the spead of coronavirus. We only allow customers who has GREEN status on their EHTERAZ APP and temperature read below 30C, this lets us ensure the safety of every valuable customer. We strive to provide the best to all our customers.


For further assistance, contact our member of staff. We are always here to help you and we hope you have a pleseant time here at our cafeteria.
